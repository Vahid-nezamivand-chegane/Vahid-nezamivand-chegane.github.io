✅ Key Advantages of Geothermal Energy

⏱ 24/7 Baseload

Independent of sunlight, wind, or rain. The most stable renewable source.

🌿 Lowest Carbon Footprint

~5% of gas plants, ~1% of coal plants. Ultra-low emissions.

🏗️ High Capacity Factor

70–90% vs. 20–30% for solar and 30–40% for wind.

💰 Low Operating Cost

Zero fuel cost, very low maintenance after initial investment.

🌍 Small Land Footprint

Much less surface area than solar farms or wind turbines.

♻️ Circular Economy

Co-extraction of lithium, gold, silver, and minerals from brine.

⚠️ Limitations of Other Renewables

☀️ Solar

Zero production at night, reduced output on cloudy days. Efficiency drops 10–20% in extreme heat. Requires expensive storage.

💨 Wind

Zero production on calm days. Highly variable output. Environmental impact on birds, noise, and visual pollution. Creates "wind shadows".

🌊 Hydropower

Dependent on rainfall – droughts reduce capacity. Massive ecosystem disruption, fish migration obstruction, and population displacement.

🎯 Geothermal is the only renewable that provides true baseload power 24/7

Without expensive batteries, without weather dependence, and with the lowest environmental impact. AquaNexus is built on this foundation.
